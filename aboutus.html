About Us Radiance Hair & Skin Clinic

Expert Care by Dr. Brijesha Chauhan

Welcome to Radiance Hair & Skin Clinic, a trusted destination for advanced dermatology, cosmetology, and aesthetic treatments. Founded by Dr. Brijesha Chauhan, a renowned expert in clinical cosmetology and trichology, our clinic is committed to providing holistic, customized care for every skin and hair concern.

Located in the heart of SURAT, Radiance Hair & Skin Clinic is equipped with the latest US-FDA approved technologies and medically proven treatments. Whether you're dealing with acne, pigmentation, hair loss, aging skin, or looking for a non-surgical transformation, we deliver safe, effective, and long-lasting results under medical supervision.

We believe beauty is not just about appearance but confidence. Our highly trained team of doctors, aestheticians, and skin therapists work together to design tailor-made solutions that align with your unique needs and lifestyle. With a warm, welcoming atmosphere and a commitment to ethical practices, Radiance has become a trusted name in skin and hair health.

Discover the new you — because your skin and hair deserve expert care.
