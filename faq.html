What services does Radiance Hair & Skin Clinic offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of advanced dermatological and aesthetic treatments including HydraFacial, Chemical Peels, Microneedling, Anti-aging treatments, Acne therapy, Pigmentation correction, PRP Hair treatment, Laser Hair Removal, Permanent Makeup, and Botox treatments. All procedures are performed under medical supervision using US-FDA approved technologies.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

Please arrive 15-20 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. This allows us to complete necessary paperwork, conduct a brief consultation, and ensure you're comfortable before beginning your treatment. For first-time patients, we recommend arriving 30 minutes early for a comprehensive skin analysis.

Are treatments safe and do they have side effects?

All our treatments are performed using US-FDA approved equipment and medically proven protocols under the supervision of Dr. Brijesha Chauhan. Minor side effects like temporary redness or mild swelling may occur with some treatments, but these typically resolve within 24-48 hours. We conduct thorough consultations to ensure treatments are suitable for your skin type.

How many sessions will I need to see results?

The number of sessions varies depending on your specific condition and treatment type. For example, HydraFacial shows immediate results, while acne treatment may require 4-6 sessions. Laser hair removal typically needs 6-8 sessions, and anti-aging treatments may require 3-5 sessions for optimal results. Dr. Chauhan will provide a personalized treatment plan during your consultation.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, any previous medical records related to skin/hair conditions, and a list of current medications or skincare products you're using. We provide all necessary amenities and post-treatment care products. If you wear makeup, we recommend coming with a clean face or we can remove it before treatment.

Do you offer consultation before treatment?

Yes, every patient receives a comprehensive consultation with Dr. Brijesha Chauhan before any treatment. This includes skin/hair analysis, discussion of your concerns and goals, explanation of suitable treatment options, and a customized treatment plan. We believe in educating our patients about their skin and hair health to achieve the best possible outcomes.

What are your clinic timings and how do I book an appointment?

We're open Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Sunday appointments are available from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM by appointment only. You can book appointments by calling +916352214662, emailing drbrijsclinic@gmail.com, or using our online booking system. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

What is your cancellation and rescheduling policy?

We request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling to avoid any charges. Same-day cancellations may incur a fee. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you to reschedule when possible. Please call us as soon as you know you need to change your appointment.

Do you provide post-treatment care instructions?

Absolutely! We provide detailed post-treatment care instructions for every procedure, including recommended skincare products, sun protection guidelines, and follow-up schedules. Our team is always available to answer any questions you may have during your recovery period. We also offer post-treatment check-ups to monitor your progress and ensure optimal results.
